Why Research Matters

In an era of information overload, the ability to synthesize knowledge quickly is a superpower.

The Research Problem

We live in a world where more information is produced every day than a person could read in a lifetime. Yet despite this abundance, quality synthesis remains scarce. Most people either skim surface-level summaries or spend hours sifting through papers they don't fully understand.

The gap between raw information and actionable insight is enormous — and it costs time, money, and opportunity every single day.

Why Deep Research Still Matters

In a world of tweets and summaries, the people who can go deep — who can read primary sources, synthesize contradictory views, and draw original conclusions — hold a significant advantage.

Researchers, strategists, investors, engineers, and policy makers all depend on quality research to make decisions that shape the world. The quality of your research directly determines the quality of your decisions.

Our Solution

ResearchAI is an autonomous multi-agent system that handles the time-consuming parts of research: finding relevant sources, filtering noise, evaluating quality, and synthesizing findings into a readable report.

It doesn't replace human judgment — it amplifies your capacity to engage with complex topics quickly and thoroughly.

Built on Proven Technology

The pipeline uses LangGraph for agent orchestration, Groq's Llama 3.3 70B for reasoning, DuckDuckGo and ArXiv for sourcing — all free, all open. This project demonstrates that cutting-edge agentic AI is accessible to anyone willing to learn.
